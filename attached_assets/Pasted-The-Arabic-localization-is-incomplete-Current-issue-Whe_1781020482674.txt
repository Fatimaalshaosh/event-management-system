The Arabic localization is incomplete.

Current issue:
When Arabic is selected, many pages still use English/LTR layout behavior.

Arabic pages must be fully RTL, not just translated text.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
GLOBAL RTL REQUIREMENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When language = Arabic:

Set:

<html dir="rtl" lang="ar">

or

document.documentElement.dir = "rtl"

The entire application should switch to RTL mode.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
LAYOUT REQUIREMENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

For Arabic:

* Page content starts from the right.
* Sidebars appear on the right side.
* Navigation flows right-to-left.
* Cards align from right to left.
* Form labels align right.
* Input text aligns right.
* Tables align right.
* Search placeholders align right.
* Modals align right.
* Dropdowns open correctly in RTL.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
TEXT ALIGNMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Arabic pages must use:

text-align: right;

for:

* Titles
* Paragraphs
* Cards
* Widgets
* Tables
* Forms
* Reports
* AI responses

No Arabic text should appear left-aligned unless explicitly required.

GRID & FLEX FIXES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Review all pages and components.

Replace layouts that are hardcoded for LTR.

Examples:

flex-row
→ flex-row-reverse in RTL

margin-left
→ margin-right in RTL

padding-left
→ padding-right in RTL

left positioning
→ right positioning in RTL
